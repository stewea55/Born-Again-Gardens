#Why It Matters
[the following is a bulleted list]
_*Church land is often underutilized.*_  Much of it remains lawn—requiring time and money to maintain—while holding the potential to be stewarded as a source of nourishment, beauty, and blessing.
_*Truly fresh produce is becoming rare.*_   Store-bought food is often far removed from harvest and grown with heavy chemical inputs, reducing its nutritional value.
_*We’re missing nature’s medicine.*_  Regular time in gardens and green spaces is proven to lower stress hormones and blood pressure while improving mental health and immune function.
_*Foundational wisdom is being lost.*_ 	Most of this generation was never taught the skills that were once passed down—how to grow food, steward land, and nourish our body.
