1. Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how Born Again Gardens uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

Born Again Gardens is the data controller for information collected through this site. Sterling Levi LLC provides website design and technical services on behalf of Born Again Gardens.

2. What are cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage or session storage used by your browser.

3. Cookies and technologies we currently use
Essential technologies

We currently use local storage and session-based technologies to:

Maintain sign-in sessions

Remember a guest cart or basket during checkout

Support basic site functionality and security

These technologies are necessary for the site to function properly.

Service provider technologies

Certain third-party service providers may set their own cookies or similar technologies as part of delivering their services, including:

Stripe (payment processing and fraud prevention)

Google (if you choose Google sign-in)

Hosting and infrastructure providers (for reliability and security)

These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

4. What we currently do not use

We do not currently use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies for behavioral advertising.

5. Your choices

You may control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain technologies may affect site functionality, including checkout and sign-in.

6. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy as our services evolve. The "Last updated" date reflects the current version.
